  1. Copy the complete prompt from the text box below.
  2. Paste it into your AI assistant.
  3. Copy the nutritional information from your source (PDF, website, etc.) and paste it directly below the prompt in the AI's input box.
  4. The AI will generate the YAML content for you.
  5. Review the generated YAML for accuracy, save it as a .yaml file, and upload it here.
