Welcome to OpenNourish!

Here are some tips to get started.

Congratulations on completing the setup! OpenNourish is ready to help you track your nutrition and achieve your health goals.

Quick Tips:
  • Log Your Food Daily: Consistency is key! Use the "Diary" section to log everything you eat and drink.
  • Search for Foods: Use the search bar to find USDA foods, or create your own custom "My Foods" and "Recipes".
  • Create Custom Recipes: Combine multiple ingredients into a single recipe to easily log meals you eat often.
  • Set and Track Goals: Monitor your progress towards your nutritional and weight goals on the Dashboard.
  • Explore Reports: Dive into detailed reports to understand your eating patterns and make informed decisions.
  • Stay Hydrated: Don't forget to log your water intake!
  • Adjust as Needed: Your goals can be updated anytime in the "Settings" section.

We recommend exploring the application to familiarize yourself with all its capabilities.
